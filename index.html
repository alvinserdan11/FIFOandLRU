FIFO

AlgorithmFirst In First Out
How it worksReplaces the page that arrived first in memory.
Best forSimple, low bookkeeping implementations.
WeaknessCan suffer from Belady's anomaly and poor performance.

LRU

AlgorithmLeast Recently Used
How it worksReplaces the page that has not been used for the longest time.
Best forWorkloads with temporal locality.
WeaknessHigher bookkeeping overhead than FIFO.
